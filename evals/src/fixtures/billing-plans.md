# Billing and Plans

TaskFlow offers three plans: Free, Team, and Business. This page covers
what each plan includes and how billing works.

## Free plan

Up to 3 teammates and 2 projects, with a 90-day activity history. No
credit card required.

## Team plan

Unlimited projects, up to 25 teammates, unlimited activity history, and
recurring subtask templates. Billed monthly per teammate.

## Business plan

Everything in Team, plus project-level access restrictions, priority
support, and a dedicated account manager. Billed annually per teammate,
with a minimum of 10 seats.

## Changing plans

Only Admins can change plans, from Settings > Billing. Upgrading takes
effect immediately; downgrading takes effect at the end of the current
billing period, and TaskFlow will warn you if your team currently
exceeds the limits of the plan you are downgrading to.

## Payment methods

TaskFlow accepts credit cards and, on the Business plan, invoicing with
net-30 terms. Update your card from Settings > Billing > Payment Method.

## Cancelling

Cancelling from Settings > Billing stops the next renewal but does not
refund the current period; your team keeps access until the period
ends, then drops to the Free plan automatically.
