# Getting Started with TaskFlow

TaskFlow is a project and task management tool for small teams. This guide
covers everything you need to create your first project and invite your
team.

## Creating your first project

From the dashboard, click "New Project" in the top left. Give it a name and
choose a color. Projects are private to your team by default; nobody
outside your organization can see them.

## Adding tasks

Open a project and click "Add Task". A task needs a title; everything else
(description, due date, assignee, labels) is optional and can be filled in
later. You can also add a task by typing a title and pressing Enter, which
creates it with default settings.

## Inviting teammates

Go to Settings > Team and click "Invite". Enter an email address and choose
a role (Member or Admin). The invite is valid for seven days. Admins can
resend or revoke a pending invite from the same screen.

## Keyboard shortcuts

Press `c` anywhere in a project to create a task. Press `/` to open search.
Press `g` then `p` to jump to the projects list.

## What's next

Once you have a project and a few tasks in it, read the Managing Tasks
guide to learn about subtasks, recurring tasks, and templates.
