# Team and Permissions

TaskFlow has two roles: Member and Admin. This page explains what each
role can do and how to change a teammate's role.

## Member permissions

Members can create, edit, and complete tasks in any project they have
been added to. Members cannot invite new teammates, change anyone's
role, or delete a project.

## Admin permissions

Admins can do everything a Member can, plus invite and remove
teammates, change roles, delete projects, and manage billing. Every
team needs at least one Admin; TaskFlow will not let you remove the
last Admin from a team.

## Changing a teammate's role

Go to Settings > Team, find the teammate, and use the role dropdown
next to their name. Only Admins can see or use this dropdown.

## Removing a teammate

Admins can remove a teammate from Settings > Team by clicking "Remove"
next to their name. Removing a teammate does not delete tasks they were
assigned; those tasks become unassigned.

## Project-level access

By default every teammate can see every project. An Admin can restrict
a specific project to a chosen list of teammates from that project's
Settings > Access tab.
