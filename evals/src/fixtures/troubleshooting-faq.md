# Troubleshooting and FAQ

## I didn't get my invite email

Check your spam folder first. Invites are valid for seven days; after
that an Admin needs to resend it from Settings > Team. If it still does
not arrive, ask an Admin to double-check the email address was typed
correctly.

## A task I created disappeared

Tasks are never deleted automatically. Check whether it was moved to a
different project, whether a filter or label view is hiding it, or
whether it was marked complete (completed tasks are hidden by default
but still visible with "Show completed" turned on).

## I can't invite new teammates

Only Admins can invite teammates. Ask an existing Admin to either send
the invite or change your role to Admin from Settings > Team.

## My recurring subtask stopped repeating

Check Settings > Templates > Rules for a conditional rule attached to
that subtask's template; a rule may be skipping new occurrences based on
a linked field, such as the assignee's "on vacation" flag.

## Notifications are not arriving

Check your personal notification settings first, then check whether
your team's Admin has disabled a notification type organization-wide
from Settings > Notifications.

## The app feels slow

TaskFlow runs entirely in your browser tab. A very large number of
open tabs, or a very old device, can slow it down. Closing unused tabs
usually helps.
