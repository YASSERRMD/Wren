# Funding Tiers

Grant size determines what else the application needs to show. This
page covers the three tiers and the prior-grant cooldown rule.

## Small grants: up to $2,000

Available to any eligible organization, with no annual budget
requirement. Small grants are meant to be a low-friction way to fund a
single, specific, immediate need.

## Standard grants: $2,001 to $10,000

Available to organizations with an annual operating budget under
$500,000. Above that budget threshold, the organization is expected to
be able to cover a request in this range from its own reserves or other
funders, so standard-tier requests from larger organizations are
generally declined.

## Large grants: $10,001 to $25,000

Available only to organizations with an annual operating budget under
$2,000,000 that have been operating for at least two years. Large
grants go through an additional review step and take longer to decide.

## Prior grant cooldown

An organization that has already received two or more grants from the
fund must wait twelve months from its most recently funded application
before applying again. This limit exists so the fund's limited budget
reaches more organizations rather than repeatedly funding the same few.
An organization with zero or one prior grant from the fund has no
cooldown restriction.
